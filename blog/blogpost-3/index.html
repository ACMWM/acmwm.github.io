Newsletter 2/10/25

This Week in ACM

ACM CS Banquet

There will be no meeting this week, instead come join us for the annual CS banquet Thursday 2/13/2025 7:30-9:30 PM in Sadler, Tidewater AB Thank you for attending the last meeting (so much fun!). Hope you had a good time making your games using APIs. This week, come eat some Peter Chang’s at the CS banquet. Whether you hope to network, socialize or just get free food, all is welcome!!. If you haven’t yet, please fill out the RSVP form below. RSVP Form

General Announcements

&hacks Organizer Application

Organizer applications for &hacks XI are open! We’re looking for committee leads and members to take &hacks to the next level. The organizing team will be composed of the following committees: Sponsorship, Finance, Marketing, Outreach, Technical, and Logistics. If you have any interest in the hackathon planning process, you have a place in the organizing team. It’s your skills and ideas that’ll make a great hackathon! (Please click the link above to fill out the form :D)

Social Event!

Join ACM for Basketball! Come with us Saturday the 15th. No skills or ability to dribble required just come out and have fun with fellow members! More information will be sent out in the groupme very exciting!

Opportunities

CS Town Hall

The CS department is planning on hosting a town hall. It is a great opportunity to connect with the CS department as they are looking to hear from a student’s perspective. The town hall will be held February 28 12-1pm McGlothlin St Hall, Room 020. (THERE WILL BE PIZZA!) This is your opportunity to speak your truth!! If you fill it out let me know and you will be awarded bisi bucks ;D Please feel free to leave your comments and concerns in the form below: Townhall for Undergraduates form

SWC Meeting!!!

On Tuesday 2/11 SWC is having their biweekly meeting! They will be going over git and Linux terminal on lab machines. Feel free to stop by (literally everyone is welcome so let’s give them some ACM support!)

Resources

CS Lab Consultants

The department provides Lab Consultants who can help with labs and homework via zoom! They meet 7-10 pm Sunday to Thursday for CSCI 300 level classes at (https://cwm.zoom.us/s/98380537213). (Don’t be afraid to reach out for help!)
